## 3. Financial Accuracy Corrections (12 changes)

### Critical Revenue Model Fixes
28. **Slide 9:** LTV changed from "$80-130" to "$240–$400 (3-5 year average customer lifetime)"
   - **Reason:** Original stated LTV was actually annual subscription value, not lifetime value. True LTV should account for multi-year retention.
   - **Impact:** More realistic investor expectations

29. **Slide 9:** LTV:CAC Ratio changed from "4.33:1" to "3.3:1 (conservative scenario)"
   - **Reason:** With corrected LTV ($240-400) and CAC ($30-120), ratio is 2:1 to 13:1. Using conservative midpoint: $300/$90 = 3.3:1
   - **Impact:** More credible unit economics

30. **Slide 9:** Conversion rate changed from "3-6% free → paid" to "0.5% at launch → 3-6% at scale"
   - **Reason:** Industry-standard meditation apps see 0.5-2% conversion in first 6 months, scaling to 3-6% over time
   - **Impact:** Sets realistic early expectations

31. **Slide 9:** Revenue Projections table completely restructured
