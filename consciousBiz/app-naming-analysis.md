## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**Top 3 Recommendations:**

1. **Rumi Sanctuary** - Best brand alignment, perfect "sanctuary not feed" positioning

**Rationale:** Perfect balance of brand philosophy, emotional resonance, differentiation, and conscious business alignment. Can optimize ASO through subtitle/keywords.

## SCORING KEY

### ASO Score (App Store Optimization)
- **9-10:** Exceptional search visibility
- **7-8:** Strong keyword strength
- **5-6:** Moderate discoverability
- **3-4:** Niche/low volume
- **1-2:** Poor searchability

### Memorability
- **Very High:** Distinctive, sticks in mind
- **High:** Easy to recall
- **Medium:** Forgettable but functional

### Brand Fit
- **Excellent:** Perfect alignment with "sanctuary, not feed" + conscious business
- **High:** Strong alignment with spiritual depth
- **Medium:** Functional but not distinctive
- **Low:** Misaligned with brand values

### Scalability
- **Excellent:** Works perfectly for Hafez, Shams, future poets
- **Good:** Adaptable with minor adjustments
- **Medium:** Requires rethinking for each poet
- **Poor:** Doesn't extend well

## TIER 1: TOP CANDIDATES (DETAILED ANALYSIS)

### 1. Rumi Daily ✅✅✅✅✅

**ASO Score: 9/10**

**Search Strength:**
- "Daily" is highly searched term in meditation/spiritual apps
- Clear value proposition: immediately communicates daily habit/practice
- Competitor comparison: "Daily Stoic" has massive success with this format
- Platform scalability: "Hafez Daily", "Shams Daily" works perfectly

**Psychology:**
- **Accessibility:** Low barrier to entry ("just one thing per day")
- **Habit formation:** Implies routine, consistency
- **Modern:** Feels contemporary, app-appropriate

**Positioning:** Approachable, habit-based spiritual practice

**Potential Concerns:**
- May feel too casual for depth seekers
- Could be confused with quote-of-the-day apps

**Verdict:** **Strong frontrunner.** Best ASO, clear positioning, proven model.

---

### 2. Rumi Sanctuary ✅✅✅✅✅

**ASO Score: 7/10**

**Search Strength:**
- "Sanctuary" has moderate search volume but high intent
- Unique in spiritual app space
- Premium positioning signal

**Psychology:**
- **Safety:** Implies refuge from noise, chaos
- **Sacred space:** Elevates beyond typical app experience
- **Calm:** Strong emotional resonance for overwhelmed users

**Positioning:** "A sanctuary, not a feed" - directly aligned with PRD

**PRD Alignment:**
- Perfect match to core philosophy
- Conscious business values embodied in name
- Differentiation from engagement-hacking competitors

**Verdict:** **Most aligned with brand philosophy.** Strong differentiation, perfect for conscious business positioning.

---

### 3. Rumi: The Masnavi ✅✅✅✅

**ASO Score: 6/10**

**Search Strength:**
- "Masnavi" has low volume but highly qualified searches
- Unique positioning: only app focused on complete Masnavi
- Educational signal: depth and authenticity

**Psychology:**
- **Authority:** Scholarly, serious, authentic
- **Exclusivity:** "The" signals definitive resource
- **Commitment:** Appeals to depth-seekers, may intimidate casual users

**Positioning:** The authoritative Masnavi resource

**Potential Concerns:**
- "Masnavi" may not be familiar to Western audiences
- Harder to explain in App Store listing
- Platform scalability unclear ("Hafez: The Divan"?)

**Verdict:** **Best for authenticity-first positioning.** Trade ASO for differentiation.

## TIER 3: POETIC BUT RISKY

### Rumi Beloved ✅✅✅
- **Authenticity:** Most authentic to Rumi's mahbub concept
- **ASO:** Very low (4/10)
- **Recommendation:** Reserve for subtitle or premium tier

### Rumi Threshold ✅✅✅✅

**ASO Score: 6/10**

**Search Strength:**
- "Threshold" has moderate search volume in spiritual/self-help category
- Strong metaphorical resonance for seekers in transition
- Unique positioning in meditation app space

**Psychology:**
- **Liminal power:** Captures spiritual transition, crossing into sacred space
- **Permission:** "Threshold" = you don't have to be perfect to enter
- **Mystery:** Intriguing, invites curiosity
- **Gentleness:** Soft entry point, non-intimidating
- **Authentic:** Persian "astan" = doorway to the sacred, deeply rooted in Rumi's tradition

**Positioning:** For seekers at the edge of transformation, ready to cross into deeper practice

**PRD Alignment:**
- Strong "sanctuary" resonance (threshold to sacred space)
- Non-manipulative, gentle invitation
- Poetic depth without pretension

**Potential Concerns:**
- May be too abstract as primary name
- Unclear functionality at first glance
- Could feel passive rather than active

**Best Use Cases:**
- **Primary name:** For premium/poetic positioning, if team prioritizes depth over ASO
- **Subtitle:** "Cross the Threshold to Rumi's Wisdom"
- **Tagline:** "At the threshold of the sacred"
- **Feature name:** "Threshold Moments" for guided contemplations

**Verdict:** **Surprisingly strong.** Beautiful poetry + authentic Sufi concept. Works as primary name for depth-first strategy, or powerful as tagline/subtitle for any choice.

### Rumi Garden ✅✅✅
- **Aesthetics:** Beautiful paradise imagery
- **Function:** Unclear, passive
- **Recommendation:** Better for visual branding

## FINAL RECOMMENDATION

### Primary Choice: **Rumi Sanctuary**

**Rationale:**
1. **Perfect brand-product fit** with PRD values ("sanctuary, not a feed")
2. **Strong differentiation** in crowded meditation/spiritual category
3. **Premium positioning** supports conscious business model and freemium strategy
4. **Emotional resonance** drives word-of-mouth and loyalty
5. **ASO optimization possible** through subtitle and keyword strategy
6. **Excellent scalability:** "Hafez Sanctuary", "Shams Sanctuary" maintains concept
7. **Conscious business embodiment:** Name itself communicates values

### Implementation Strategy:

**App Store Title:**
`Rumi Sanctuary - Daily Masnavi Wisdom`

**In-App Branding:**
Rumi Sanctuary (consistent)

**Marketing Tagline:**
"A sanctuary for Rumi's wisdom. Not a feed, a refuge."

**Investor Deck Positioning:**
"The only sanctuary for authentic Rumi wisdom in a world of fragmented quotes"
